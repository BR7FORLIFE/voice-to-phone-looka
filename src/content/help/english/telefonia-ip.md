<summary>What should an ideal IP telephony system look like?</summary>

What should an ideal IP telephony system look like?

An ideal IP telephony system should include the following key points:

1. An intuitive interface and easy configuration.
2. Adaptability to the company's changing needs.
3. Security.
4. Advanced calling features.
5. Good technical support.
6. Integration with other communication tools.

</details>

<details id="telefonia-ip-q-1-como-es-la-calidad-del-sonido-con-la-telefonia-ip">
<summary>How is the sound quality with IP telephony?</summary>

How is the sound quality with IP telephony?

Sound quality is excellent and clear, assuming a good internet service and well-distributed internal network usage. Therefore, call quality depends on the quality of the internet service.

</details>

<details id="telefonia-ip-q-2-son-gratis-las-llamadas-realizadas-por-telefonia-ip">
<summary>Are calls made via IP telephony free?</summary>

Are calls made via IP telephony free?

No, they are not free, as interconnection fees to local telecom operators apply in each country. However, they are much more economical than traditional telephony.

</details>

<details id="telefonia-ip-q-3-son-seguras-las-llamadas-por-telefonia-ip">
<summary>Are calls over IP telephony secure?</summary>

Are calls over IP telephony secure?

Calls over IP telephony are encrypted, unlike traditional telephony. Therefore, they can be considered secure.

</details>

<details id="telefonia-ip-q-4-que-importancia-tiene-telefonia-ip-en-un-pbx-virtual">
<summary>What importance does IP telephony have in a Virtual PBX?</summary>

What importance does IP telephony have in a Virtual PBX?

IP telephony plays a very important role in a [cloud PBX](https://www.voicetophone.com/centralita-virtual-que-es-y-como-funciona/es) or virtual PBX because it helps manage and route calls, queues, and other functionalities. It also helps interconnect SIP trunks with other VoIP providers and PSTN links to reduce call and infrastructure costs.

</details>

Do I need to buy physical equipment to launch the IP telephony service?

No, it is not necessary to invest in physical equipment to launch your telephone system. If you require a cloud telephone system, you will not need to worry about installation or maintenance costs; Voicetophone will maintain your service optimally 365 days a year.

Extensions can be used with softphones for PCs and mobile devices, allowing you to receive and answer calls conveniently while working.

Physical equipment should only be purchased if the customer requests it for preference or a special requirement.

</details>
